Coronavirus Disease 2019 (Covid-19)

Situation Report-115

Delta State Government

Data as received by Delta State Ministry of Health 20:00 NST 30

Background

  • On 07 April, 2020, Delta State recorded the first confirmed case of COVID-19
  • The index case was a male aged 50 years with travel history to Canada (date of return 06 Feb, 2020), and then out-of-state travel to Abuja (12-13 Mar, 2020)
  • Till date, 1502 cases have been confirmed. 1370 cases have been discharged 42 deaths recorded in Delta State
  • A multi-sectoral statewide emergency operation team has been activated and coordinates the states response activities
  • The following is an epidemiological report on COVID-19 in the State. It is the 115 th daily report since the index case in the State and provides update on the situation with the epidemic and outlines key activities undertaken on 30 th July, 2020

HIGHLIGHTS FROM PILLARS/OPERATIONAL UNITS

Surveillance
  • Investigated 98 new suspect cases in 7 LGAs
  • Confirmed 16 new COVID-19 cases in 4 LGAs
  • Identified and linelisted 20 new contacts
Ambulance and Transport Services
  • Facilitated transport of samples from 6 Health Facilities and home isolation in Asaba to ASH, Asaba
Call Centre
